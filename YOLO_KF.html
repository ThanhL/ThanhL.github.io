Deep Learning Networks with Kalman Filter Demo

During my experience at Accenture, I was tasked to build a people and object tracking algorithm for a demo to showcase for a particular client. Given the low resolution of the video stream, approaches purely based on AI algorithms would fail due to the low quality of the video stream. As a proof of concept, I was tasked to build a tracking algorithm given these constraints. By using kalman filters fused with a YOLO detector (a deep learning based detector), tracking was achieved with fairly decent results.

More imporvements can be made for the Kalman Filter + YOLO tracker, such as using Unscented Kalman Filters to deal with the nonlinearities of people moving. Futher tuning of the parameters could also be applied in future work of this demo.
