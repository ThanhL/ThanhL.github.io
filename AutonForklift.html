Autonomous Forklift Navigation (Master's Thesis)

The Autonomous Forklift Navigation was my Master's Thesis investigating safe autonomous navigation of a forklift around a structured warehouse environment. As part of the thesis, we explored path planning algorithms that incorporated the dynamics of the forklift while taking into account obstacle avoidance and trajectory tracking control. Many path planning algorithms were explored, but ultimately Hybrid A* integrated with Dubins Path was used as the vehicle dynamics can incorporated easily. Once the trajectories were calculated from Hybrid A*, the trajectories were then followed using PID control.

A simulation demonstrating the autonomous capability was devoloped in Unity. The dynamics of the forklift and the path planning were incorporated into the simulation, which was coded in C#.
