About Me

I'm a mechatronics engineer who loves to tinker and experiment with robotic systems! I graduated from the University of Melbourne with a Bachelor of Science majoring in Electrical Sytems and a Masters Degree in Mechatronics. In my spare time, I enjoy working on self funded robotics projects, with a strong interest in mobile robotics and deep learning.

Robotics

Experience with designing, building and programming robotic systems. Familiar with Control algorithms and experience with Mobile Robotics algorithms including SLAM and Path Planning.

Programming

Proficient in Python, C, C++, MATLAB and Object Oriented Programming. Familiar with Linux and minor experience in networking/security. Experience with code collaboration and git.

Machine Learning/Deep Learning

Familiar with machine learning and deep learning algorithms. Experience with writing algorithms with Tensorflow, Keras, Scikit Learn and Pandas.

Electronics

Experience with designing and implementing electronics with Arduino, Raspberry PI, and various microcontrollers. Proficient with Altium Design, PCB construction, electrical debugging and hardware programming.

Mechanical Design

Familiar with CAD software such as Autodesk Inventor. Experience in rapidly producing and testing mechanical protypes with personal 3D printer.

Machine Assembly/Machine maintenance

Proficient with power tools, machine assembly and maintenance. Experience with building computers and 3D printers.

Computer Vision

Experience with OpenCV and computer vision algorithms.
