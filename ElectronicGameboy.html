Electronic Gameboy

The Electric Gameboy was part of my Masters course in which students design and build a programmable embedded device that can play simple games. Each student was given a kit of starting materials consisting of electrical components such as resisitors, capacitors, diodes, etc.

A PCB was designed in Altium Designer and fabricated from the ground up. The electronic components were then soldered on and incrementally tested. Once everything was assembled, through AVR programming, a simple game was coded up in C.
